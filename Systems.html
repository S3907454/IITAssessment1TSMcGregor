Project

Systems and Equipment

We will be developing a Hive programming and triggering mechanism that would link the drones to a parent system, usually linked to a mobile vehicle in the area. Depending on the need of the drone, the programming can be adjusted for either just search or it can also include the addition of when a certain parameter is met, would then release the package, or trigger the fire extinguisher to or near its target.

This can be an automatic process, or the release and flight of the drone be controlled manually.

The drones would come with a HD camera with heat sensing ability.

A lightweight frame with a release mechanism built onto the underneath of the drone would be where various attachments can be made depending on the requirements at that time, be it a fire extinguisher, safety packages or flotation devices.

The drones can carry approx. 20 to 30 kgs, so carrying a small to medium fire extinguisher would be no problem.

For the parent vehicles, pending on the size of the vehicle, they could host from 3 to 5 drones depending on the needs.

A lightweight frame could be easily manufactured to suit the vehicle, much like a roof rack on a 4wd.

The drones can be charged either by portable solar panels or via the vehicles car battery if required.

For a helicopter, the lightweight frame could be fitted to the under carriage between the legs of the vehicle and the drones released when needed.
